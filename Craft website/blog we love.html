      Join us as a CPI Creator!

      CPI Creators will receive regular “Happy Mail” packages filled with craft materials! Each cycle, you will create one (or more) craft projects with your Happy Mail goodies & share them on your website or social media pages. We will then post a step-by-step tutorial CraftProjectIdeas.com linking to your website.

      Join us as a Giveaway Guru!

      Giveaway Gurus will receive occasional “Happy Mail” packages filled with materials to create a craft, plus one pack to give away to your followers! Use your social media guru skills to engage your fans and followers to enter to win the craft pack. Once the winner is chosen, the Craft Project Ideas team will ship a bunch of craft goodies to your winner so they can recreate your craft!
